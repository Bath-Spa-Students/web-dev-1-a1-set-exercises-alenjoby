Highlights

Setting and atmosphere

  • The movie transports the viewer to Konkan coast, A peacefull place with natural beauty.
  • The Color palette used (blue) reinforces the calm atmosphere, offering a sense of escape and new beginnings

Characters

  • Manu : The Central Character Who is waiting to living with the love of his life (priya).
  • Priya : A College student with dreams of having a simple life with "Manu". She offers Manu a chance at happiness and connection.
  • Supporting Cast : Side A may introduce local villagers, a mentor figure, or a point of contention; nevertheless, Side B reveals the true significance of these Characters.

Plot Points

  1. The Focus is on Manu's journey of Self-Discovery and escape in the Konkan Area.
  2. By establishing a bond between Manu and Priya, the movie gives viewers a ray of hope for the future.
  3. Signs of Manu's internal conflicts and outside influences point to possible difficulties in the future.
  4. Viewers are hungry for Side B as Manu wrestles with his past and negotiates his new connection after Side A ends with a sense of hope laced with uncertanity.
