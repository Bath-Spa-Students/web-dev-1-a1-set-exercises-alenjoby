Cheesy Fries

Golden-brown french fires are topped with melted, creamy cheese to make a Cheesy fries, a delicious comfort dish. Crispy fries are a perfect vessel for a creamy cheesesauce or a combination of mozerella and cheddar. with an explosion of flavors and sensation in every bite, cheesy fries are elavated with toppings like crispy bacon, fresh green onions, and jalepenos. With it's tempting combination of crunchy, cheesy goodness, this delicious delight connects people whether it's eaten as a side dish, appetizer, or snack.

Ingredients

  • 32 ounces frozen french fries
  • 2 cups shredded cheddar cheese
  • 1 cup shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 3/4 cup bacon cooked and crumbled
  • 1/4 cup green onion sliced
  • 2 tablespoons parsley chopped
  • 3/4 cup ranch dressing

Methods

  1. Cook the french fries in the oven on a sheet pan according to package direction
  2. Remove the pan of fries from the oven. Turn the oven heat to 400 degrees F.
  3. You can finish the fries on a sheet pan, or transfer them to a skillet for a restaurant style presentation.
  4. Sprinle the cheese over the fries.
  5. Return the fries to the oven. Bake for 5 minutes or untill cheese is melted.
  6. Sprinle the bacon, green onions and parsley over the fries
  7. Serve immediately with ranch dressing on the side, or alternatively you can drizzle the ranch over the fries.
